About Us

The team behind HarmonyBot

Our Story

Founded in 2025, HarmonyBot was created with a vision to strengthen human relationships through the power of AI—whether between partners, friends, colleagues, or families.

Our diverse team of experts in AI, psychology, education, design, and business comes from all around the world, united by one mission: to use technology to foster empathy, emotional growth, and better communication in everyday life.

Among our innovations is Timi—a friendly AI-powered robot designed to support children’s emotional and social development. Timi brings playful interaction, thoughtful conversation, and psychological insight to the heart of childcare, while keeping parents connected and informed.

Our Mission

At HarmonyBot, our mission is to make relationship support accessible and meaningful for everyone. Whether it's helping resolve conflict or building stronger family bonds, we create tools that improve emotional well-being—starting with children and growing into every human connection.

Our Values

  • Empathy: We create AI that listens, understands, and supports emotional needs.
  • Innovation: We harness technology to improve human connection—not replace it.
  • Privacy: We safeguard every user's personal space and data with care and respect.
  • Inclusivity: Our global team designs for diverse cultures, backgrounds, and experiences.
  • Science-driven: Our products are grounded in psychology, education, and relationship research.
