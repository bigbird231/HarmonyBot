What is HarmonyBot?

HarmonyBot is a forward-thinking technology company focused on creating emotionally intelligent robots that support human relationships — especially within families.

Founded on the belief that technology can bring people closer, HarmonyBot designs friendly, AI-powered companions that help children feel understood, help parents stay connected, and help families grow in harmony.

  • Timi A childcare robot designed to talk, play, and emotionally engage with children while supporting parents with gentle insights.
  • Harmony Our first-generation relationship robot, built to assist in emotional support and daily communication.

How Can Timi Help You?

Whether you're cooking, working, or just need a short break, Timi is there to keep your child company. Here's what Timi can do:

  • Talk and Listen: Timi chats naturally with children, helping them express their thoughts and feelings.
  • Play and Learn: Timi can tell stories, sing songs, play games, and even teach basic skills in a fun way.
  • Support Emotional Health: Timi understands simple emotional signals and can guide children with kindness.
  • Help Parents Stay Involved: You can review conversations, set limits, and receive gentle insights into how your child is feeling.

Timi isn’t just a robot — it’s a thoughtful friend for your child, and a helpful partner for you.

How Can Harmony Help You?

Harmony is designed to assist with a wide range of relationship challenges:

  • Romantic Relationships: Navigate disagreements, improve communication, and strengthen bonds with your partner.
  • Family Dynamics: Resolve conflicts between parents and children, siblings, or extended family members.
  • Workplace Relations: Mediate tensions between colleagues, improve team dynamics, and create a more harmonious work environment.
  • Friendship Issues: Address misunderstandings and conflicts between friends.

By providing an objective third-party perspective and evidence-based conflict resolution strategies, Harmony helps people build stronger, healthier relationships.
